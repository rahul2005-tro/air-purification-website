Why This Project Matters

Every year, Delhi-NCR faces dangerously high pollution levels due to vehicular emissions, stubble burning, and industrial activity. Large monitoring stations exist — but they are too few and too expensive for localized use.

Our Vision

We aim to decentralize clean-air access by combining IoT, solar power, and smart sensors into affordable, community-level solutions.

"Clean air should not be a privilege — it should be a right."

How Our System Works

Smart Sensors

Measure PM2.5, PM10, CO, NOx, SOx, and VOCs in real time.

AI-Based Source Detection

Uses ML models to identify pollution sources (traffic, stubble burning, industrial).

Adaptive Filtration

Intelligent HEPA + Activated Carbon filters adjust performance based on air quality data.

IoT Connectivity

Sends real-time air quality data to a cloud dashboard via Wi-Fi or LoRaWAN.

Renewable Power

Operates on solar energy with lithium-ion battery backup for off-grid use.

"Every breath counts — every sensor matters."

Our Impact Goals

✅ Empower citizens with affordable localized clean-air solutions.
✅ Reduce immediate exposure to high-risk groups (children, elderly, patients).
✅ Generate granular pollution maps to identify hyperlocal sources.
✅ Develop IoT-enabled low-cost purifier + sensor units.
✅ Integrate solar and battery backup systems.
✅ Conduct field tests in high-pollution zones for performance validation.

Our systems have already reduced PM2.5 levels by up to 25% in pilot tests at selected Delhi schools.
